Dill Gazpacho

Description

This is a refreshing and flavorful cold soup, perfect for a hot summer day. The combination of fresh vegetables and dill creates a unique and delicious taste.

It's quick to prepare and requires no cooking, making it an ideal choice for a healthy and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed peeled cucumbers
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine cucumbers, broth, sour cream, dill, vinegar and garlic.
  2. Cover and process until smooth.
  3. Transfer to a large bowl; stir in salt and pepper.
  4. Refrigerate, covered, until chilled.
  5. Serve cold.
