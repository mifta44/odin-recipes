Fluffy Pancakes

Description

A simple, no-fail recipe for light and fluffy pancakes that are perfect for a weekend breakfast. This recipe uses common ingredients you likely already have in your kitchen. Serve them warm with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

The secret to fluffy pancakes is not to overmix the batter; a few lumps are perfectly fine!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until just smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
